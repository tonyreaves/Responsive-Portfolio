My story

After a decade in journalism, I left to pursue coding. I've been a reporter, a copy editor, a web editor and a social media manager. At some point, I realized building things was the most rewarding part of the job, and that learning to code was becoming less and less intimidating.


I've already built several pages and apps. You can check them out on my portfolio page. I'm hoping to keep building my skills at the same rate I have since I began coding bootcamp.
